Welcome!
 Monsoon is a family owned-and-operated habitat and petting zoo!
We are certified by the AWBI and have routine inspections,
so please do not stress and enjoy your visit!

We offer...
  • Self-guided tours of the property
  • An opportunity to interact with the friendly animals.
  • An immersive experience to sit inside of a bird cage
    and hand-feed the parakeets.
  • Hand-feed our fish at no extra cost.
  • Homestyle Lunch and Dinner available with reservation.
